Quiclipper FAQ

  • What is Quiclipper?

    Quiclipper is a Safari web extension that helps you clip web content to you favorite notes app.

  • Why do I need to use Quiclipper?

    When you find some great content on web page and want to save it to your notes app, it is difficult to just pick up the major content and remove the ads, recommend information, comments from other people, etc. Then Quiclipper will do all the things for you in just a few clicks.

  • How do I install Quiclipper?

    Just like the other apps, you can go to Apple App Store to get Quiclipper.

  • How do I use Quiclipper?

    After you install Quiclipper and enable it in Safari Settings, there will be an icon on the toolbar of Safari. Just click on it when you want to clip something.

  • What is privacy?

    We DO NOT collect any information about you. You can get more information in our Privacy Policy.
